Introducing SCAMP-5c


SCAMP-5 Vision Chip

A image sensor with a parallel processer on focal plane.


SCAMP-5c System Architecture

IPU + PE

IPU execute ipu instructions like a common micro-controller. Upon a PE instruction, IPU issues the PE instruction to all of the 256x256 PEs in the SCAMP-5 chip.
